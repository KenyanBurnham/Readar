Readar


This is a toolset that allows users to gather statistics on bodies of text.

How it works.


Below is a general breakdown of Readar's functionality:


❶ Parses text and segments paragraphs based on carriage returns.

❷ Segments paragraph into sentences by "." + "SPACE" pairs.

❸ Segments sentences into words by whitespace (TABS and SPACES).
