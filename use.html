Use Readar


How to use: Enter in text and press "submit".



 


 

Guidance


Statistics generated by this app only apply within the specific text itself. Though sentences are built from common materials (i.e. characters, words, the English language, etc.), data generated about sentences are not entirely useful when comparing two different texts.

Remember that all rhetoric has a rhetorical situation in which it is written. Comparing bodies of text require's consideration about the the authors intents, backgrounds, and the contexts in which the texts are written.

In future revisions of Readar, objective information will be comparable by normalizing the data (putting the data on the same scale).

To better understand Readar, see some of the samples provided.
